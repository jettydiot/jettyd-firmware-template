Flash your ESP32-C3

No toolchain required. One click installs the jettyd firmware and starts Wi-Fi provisioning — all from your browser.

Before you start

  • Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge (WebSerial is not supported in Firefox or Safari)
  • Connect your ESP32-C3 to your computer with a data-capable USB cable (not a charge-only cable)
  • Close any serial monitors or other programs that may be using the port
  • Have your jettyd fleet token ready for provisioning
ESP32-C3 · jettyd firmware

Requires a browser with WebSerial API support. If the button does not appear, check that you are using Chrome or Edge.

After flashing

1
Wi-Fi provisioning

The device will prompt you to enter your network credentials via the Improv Wi-Fi dialog that appears automatically after flashing.

2
Enter your fleet token

Once online, the device calls home. Paste your ft_… token from app.jettyd.com when prompted.

3
Device appears online

Head to app.jettyd.com → Devices — your device should appear within a few seconds.
